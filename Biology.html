Advanced topics in biology, including plant and animal biology, cellular and molecular biology with a focus on DNA processes and gene expression, and environmental biology, examining the impact of human activities on the environment and principles of conservation.

Advanced Placement (AP) Biology (30/35)

An in-depth study of biology at a college level, covering biochemistry and cellular structure and function, advanced genetics and evolutionary processes, ecology with an emphasis on population dynamics, and physiology exploring cellular energetics and homeostasis.
